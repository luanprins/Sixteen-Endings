This game is about seeing how far branching story paths can be taken.

Absurdly subtle changes in choice have immense gravity in the storyline. It is both an ode to, and satire of, games emphasizing player choice, like The Stanley Parable meets
The Walking Dead.

HOW TO PLAY

Currently, running the script requires Python to be installed on your machine.

Then just run the main file making sure to have the accompanying Dimensions folder in the same directory.

The game is entirely based on numeric inputs which you use to choose your next move from a list.

HOW TO GET EVERY ENDING

The ultimate goal of Sixteen Endings is to collect all sixteen endings and become a completionist God!

Here is how:

Ending 1: Scream at the bear and get your legs eaten off

Ending 2: Try to rip the bear's face off right after it ate yours off and get disemboweled

Ending 3: Get your face eaten off and don't replace it before going through door 4

Ending 4: Go to the dance wearing the bear's face and the necklace

Ending 5: Go to the dance wearing the bear's face, but not the necklace

Ending 6: Go to the dance wearing the necklace, but not the bear's face

Ending 7: Go to the dance wearing neither, and having killed the bear

Ending 8: Go to the dance having put the bear to sleep and carrying the wire

Ending 9: Go to the dance having put the bear to sleep and not carrying the wire

Ending 10: Go to Duchamp wearing the bear's face then spend your time in misery at the asylum

Ending 11: Go to Duchamp wearing the bear's face then bribe asylum staff with the tooth or necklace

Ending 12: Go to Duchamp wearing the bear's face then kill asylum staff with the wire or necklace

Ending 13: Go to Duchamp having killed the bear, but not wearing its face, and carrying the wire/necklace

Ending 14: Go to Duchamp having killed the bear, but not wearing its face, and not carrying the wire/necklace

Ending 15: Go to Duchamp having put the bear to sleep and carrying the wire

Ending 16: Go to Duchamp having put the bear to sleep and not carrying the wire
